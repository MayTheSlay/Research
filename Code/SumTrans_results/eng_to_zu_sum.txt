Document 1
Ukunakekelwa kwezempilo okuyisisekelo kuyindlela yomphakathi wonke yokuhlela ngempumelelo nokuqinisa izinhlelo zezempilo zikazwelonke ukuletha izinsizakalo zezempilo nenhlalakahle eduze nemiphakathi. Iqukethe izingxenye ezintathu: izinsizakalo zezempilo ezihlanganisiwe ukuhlangabezana nezidingo zezempilo zabantu kukho konke ukuphila kwabo ukubhekana nezimo ezibanzi zezempilo ngenqubomgomo yezinhlaka eziningi kanye nezenzo ezinika abantu amandla, emindenini nasemiphakathini ukuzibophezela empilweni yabo. Umthelela: Ukutholakala kwezempilo emhlabeni wonke kuzokwenzeka kuphela uma wonke umuntu, yonke indawo ingakwazi ukufinyelela ezikhungweni zezempilo ezizidingayo. Ukwanda kokungenelela kokunakekelwa kwezempilo okuyisisekelo emazweni anemali ephansi naphakathi nendawo kungasindisa izimpilo eziyizigidi ezingama-60 futhi kwandise isilinganiso sokuphila ngeminyaka engu-3.7 ngo-2030. Ngonyaka olandelayo, emhlanganweni wezinga eliphezulu we-UN mayelana nokutholakala kwezempilo kuwo wonke umuntu, Amazwe aqinisekise ukuzibophezela kwawo ekwandiseni ukuhlinzekwa nokubeka phambili ukunakekelwa kwezempilo okuyisisekelo njengetshe legumbi lomhlaba ogxile kubantu, uhlelo lwezempilo oluhlangene nolusekelwe emphakathini kanye nesisekelo sokufinyelela ekuvikelekeni kwezempilo emhlabeni jikelele.

Omunye wemisebenzi eyinhloko yeNhlangano ukubeka imigomo yamazwe omhlaba, izindinganiso neziqondiso, kubandakanya ukubeka izindinganiso zomhlaba wonke zocwaningo.
